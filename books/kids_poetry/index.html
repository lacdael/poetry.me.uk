Kids Poetry

This is a collection of metered rhyming poems for children. Each poem is accompanied by an illustration.

The book is comprised of 32 metered pieces of kids rhyming poetry, and 32 accompanying illustrations.

Poetry for children offers another valuable opportunity for children to interact with reading. Poetry can act as a middle ground between nursery rhymes and reading books.
