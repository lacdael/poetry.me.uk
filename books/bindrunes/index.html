Bindrunes

A collection of metered rhyming poems about bindrune spells, intended to uplift and help manifest a better reality.

There are poems or spells for all manner of situations. There are poems for strength, poems for protection, and poems of affirmations of potential.

This collection is written for anyone interested in the culture of the native Europeans, or anyone for whom the spells or poems can uplift and inspire.

This poetry collection is dedicated to every child of Woden.
